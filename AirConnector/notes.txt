The AirConnectorPin is glued into the AirConnector this is used to push the perfect draft valve to allow air (in/out) of the keg.

The 6mm clear tubing is pushed onto the end of the air connector and this is used to connect to the vacuum pump box.

The bracket is used to hold the airconnector in place when in use.

Note: Use lubricate on the O-ring to help with the airconnector.
